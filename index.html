Akan Naming Algorithm

This is a fun naming app that takes your birth date as input and tells you on which week day you were born and your corresponding Akan name. Akan naming is a part of the Ghanian culture of child-naming.

Curious already?

In every African culture it is tradition to name the child immediately after it is born. Most communities have very specific methods of name choosing, mostly revolving around seasons and other natural phenomena. The Akan of Ghana name their children according to the day of the week they were born.
